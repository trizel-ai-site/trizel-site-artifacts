Governance Summary

TRIZEL's three-layer architecture ensures institutional accountability and scientific rigor

Three-Layer Architecture

Layer-0 (Governance): Establishes policies, approves artifacts, maintains final authority. Authority flows from trizel-core governance repository.

Layer-1 (Execution): Internal research, analysis, and validation. Private repositories perform scientific work under Layer-0 authorization.

Layer-2 (Presentation): This repository‚Äîpublic-facing display of approved content only. No authority, no execution, no live data.
