What is the GOI?

The Global Observation & Production Index (GOI) serves as TRIZEL's canonical publication registry‚Äîa structured, multilingual catalog of verified scientific artifacts. Unlike traditional academic repositories, the GOI explicitly separates observation (data sources) from production (analytical outputs), ensuring clear provenanceThe documented origin and chain of custody for all scientific data and outputs. and institutional accountability.

Key principles:

  • Evidence-first: All entries include verifiable metadata and frozen snapshots
  • Layer separation: Clear distinction between governance, execution, and presentation
  • Multilingual access: Full documentation in 6 languages (EN/FR/DE/RU/ZH/AR)
  • Static registry: No dynamic updates‚Äîall changes require governance approval
