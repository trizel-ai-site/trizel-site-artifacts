Publication Readiness Disclaimer

This directory establishes publication readiness rules and governance frameworks ONLY.

This section contains NO scientific results, NO experimental data, NO performance metrics, NO analytical findings, and NO empirical claims.

TRIZEL is a governance and methodological framework. This documentation describes organizational structure and publication policies. It does not constitute scientific publication or research output.

Purpose

This section establishes the scientific publication readiness framework for the TRIZEL project. It clarifies what TRIZEL is, what it is not, and how any future scientific work conducted within TRIZEL governance will be handled with respect to external publication.

What TRIZEL Is

TRIZEL is an institutional governance and methodological framework that establishes:

  • A three-layer separation architecture for governance, research, and presentation
  • Organizational policies for managing research provenance and documentation
  • Institutional boundaries between internal operations and public presentation
  • Methodological principles for maintaining separation of concerns

What TRIZEL Is Not

TRIZEL is explicitly not:

  • A scientific research paper or publication
  • An experimental platform or data analysis system
  • A source of scientific claims or empirical findings
  • A repository of research results or performance metrics
  • A preprint server or scientific communication platform

No Scientific Content

This website contains no scientific content.

No materials on this site constitute scientific findings, research results, experimental data, or analytical conclusions. All content is institutional documentation describing organizational structure and governance frameworks.

Framework vs. Research

TRIZEL distinguishes clearly between framework and research:

Framework (This Site)

This website documents TRIZEL's organizational framework, including governance structures, methodological principles, and institutional boundaries. This documentation is descriptive and non-authoritative.

Research (External Publication)

Any scientific research conducted under TRIZEL governance occurs within internal Layer-1 operations. Research findings, if deemed suitable for external publication, will be submitted to peer-reviewed journals, conferences, or other established scientific venues following standard academic publication processes.

No research results appear on this website. This site serves only as institutional documentation of the governance framework itself.

Future Publication Pathway

Should TRIZEL-governed research produce scientifically significant results, those results will be:

  1. Subjected to internal review and validation within Layer-1 operations
  2. Prepared according to target journal or conference standards
  3. Submitted to external peer-reviewed publication venues
  4. Published externally first, through traditional academic channels
  5. Referenced (but not reproduced) on this website only after external publication

This website will never serve as the primary publication venue for scientific research. All scientific claims and findings will be published externally through peer-reviewed channels before any reference appears here.

No Preprint or Working Paper Status

This website is not a preprint server, working paper repository, or unpublished research archive. Materials presented here are institutional documentation only, not scientific manuscripts or research reports.

TRIZEL does not claim preprint status, does not request citation as a research publication, and does not position itself as a contribution to scientific literature.
