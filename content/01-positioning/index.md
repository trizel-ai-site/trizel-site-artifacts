## What TRIZEL Is

TRIZEL is an Institutional Scientific Observatory.

It operates as a **standard layer** between data providers and interpretation.

Its role is to present governance-approved, static scientific artifacts in a structured, traceable, and reproducible form.

The canonical flow is:

```
Data Providers → TRIZEL → Interpretation
```

TRIZEL occupies the mediation layer. It does not originate data. It does not produce interpretation.

---

## What TRIZEL Is Not

- **Not a data provider.** TRIZEL does not generate or collect raw data.
- **Not an inference engine.** TRIZEL performs no analysis, inference, or derived conclusions.
- **Not an interactive analysis tool.** No dashboards, no queries, no live computation.
- **Not an AI assistant.** No conversational interface, no recommendation system.
- **Not a publication platform.** TRIZEL presents artifacts; it does not author them.
