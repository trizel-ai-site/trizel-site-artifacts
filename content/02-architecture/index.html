System Architecture

Layer-0 — Governance / Constitutional Authority

Establishes institutional policies and governance rules. Approves artifacts before publication. Maintains final and binding authority. Source: trizel-core.

Layer-1 — Execution / Analysis

Internal research, analysis, and validation performed under Layer-0 authorization. Scientific work is private; outputs flow to Layer-2 only after governance approval.

Layer-2 — Site Surface (this layer)

Public-facing, read-only presentation of approved artifacts. No authority, no execution, no live data, no inference.

Separation of Concerns

  • Layer-2 does not execute analysis (Layer-1 concern)
  • Layer-2 does not establish policy (Layer-0 concern)
  • Layer-1 does not publish directly (requires Layer-0 approval)
  • Layer-0 does not perform computation (Layer-1 concern)
