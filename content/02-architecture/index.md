## Epistemic Layer Structure

TRIZEL operates across three distinct layers with explicit separation of concerns:

### Layer-0 — Governance / Constitutional Authority

- Establishes institutional policies and governance rules
- Approves artifacts before publication
- Maintains final and binding authority over all outputs
- Source: `trizel-core` governance repository

### Layer-1 — Execution / Analysis

- Internal research, analysis, and validation
- Scientific work performed under Layer-0 authorization
- Private repositories; outputs flow to Layer-2 only after governance approval

### Layer-2 — Site Surface (this layer)

- Public-facing, read-only presentation of approved artifacts
- No authority, no execution, no live data, no inference
- Governed by `SITE_DEPENDENCY_ORDER.md` and related Layer-2 governance documents

---

## Separation of Concerns

No layer may assume the responsibilities of another:

- Layer-2 does not execute analysis (Layer-1 concern)
- Layer-2 does not establish policy (Layer-0 concern)
- Layer-1 does not publish directly (requires Layer-0 approval)
- Layer-0 does not perform computation (Layer-1 concern)
