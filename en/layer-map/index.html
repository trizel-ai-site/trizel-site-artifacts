Layer Descriptions

Layer-0 — Governance & Authority

Layer-0 is the canonical governance layer. It holds final authority over all policies, standards, gate transitions, publication approvals, and organizational decisions within TRIZEL. No scientific result or publication is valid without Layer-0 authorization.

Repository: trizel-ai-site/trizel-core

Layer-1 — Laboratory Execution Domain

Layer-1 is the private laboratory execution domain. It hosts all research algorithms, data pipelines, claim verification workflows, and scientific computation. Results are published to Layer-2 only after passing all gate checks and receiving Layer-0 publication authorization.

Primary account: abdelkader-omran (Layer-1 execution account)

Layer-2 — Institutional Presentation

Layer-2 is the public-facing presentation layer. It hosts this website (trizel-ai.com), all static institutional pages, multilingual content, the GOI, and the Epistemic Atlas. Layer-2 performs no analysis, no computation, and has no execution authority. All content is pre-approved and static.

Repository: trizel-ai-site/trizel-site-artifacts (this repository)
