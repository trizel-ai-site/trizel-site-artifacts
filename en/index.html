Welcome to TRIZEL

TRIZEL is an institutional scientific observatory operating under strict governance protocols. This system implements a layered architecture separating governance (Layer-0), execution (Layer-1), and presentation (Layer-2).

Mission

To provide transparent, governance-controlled access to scientific research outputs and institutional processes through a multi-layered architecture that ensures separation of concerns and institutional accountability.

Governance Framework

All operations are governed by the TRIZEL Governance Framework, which defines:

  • Layer-0: Constitutional governance and immutable rules
  • Layer-1: Execution layer with private repositories and controlled access
  • Layer-2: Public presentation layer (this site)
