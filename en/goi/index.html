General Object Index (GOI)

Overview

The General Object Index (GOI) is TRIZEL's structured catalog of research artifacts, documentation, and institutional objects. GOI provides standardized access to research outputs through a governance-controlled indexing system.

Purpose

GOI serves as the primary interface between Layer-1 execution and Layer-2 presentation, ensuring that all published research artifacts meet governance standards and are properly documented.

Structure

GOI organizes research artifacts by:

  • Research domains and categories
  • Temporal organization (phases, milestones)
  • Governance status and approval state
  • Multilingual access paths
