# EXCLUSIONS

## Prohibited Content and Activities

This document explicitly defines all content types and activities that are **strictly prohibited** in this Layer-2 presentation repository.

## Scientific Claims

**PROHIBITED:**
- Research assertions or conclusions
- Scientific hypotheses or theories
- Experimental findings or results
- Analytical outcomes or interpretations
- Scientific methodologies or protocols
- Validation claims or verification statements
- Quality assessments or performance claims

**No authority** — Layer-2 cannot make scientific statements.

**No results** — Layer-2 cannot publish research outcomes.

## Research Interpretation

**PROHIBITED:**
- Interpretation of Layer-1 research findings
- Analysis of experimental results
- Commentary on research quality or significance
- Synthesis of research conclusions
- Evaluation of research methodologies
- Assessment of research implications
- Technical analysis or review

**No interpretation** — Layer-2 has no interpretative power.

**Non-scientific** — Layer-2 does not engage with research content.

## DOI Usage

**PROHIBITED:**
- Assignment of Digital Object Identifiers (DOIs)
- Registration of research artifacts
- Creation of citable persistent identifiers
- Publication of DOI-referenced materials
- Management of DOI metadata
- DOI-based archival activities

**No citation** — Layer-2 materials are not citable research.

**No publication** — Layer-2 does not publish research artifacts.

## Zenodo Usage

**PROHIBITED:**
- Deposit of research materials to Zenodo
- Creation of Zenodo records or versions
- Publication of datasets or code via Zenodo
- Management of Zenodo metadata
- Use of Zenodo for archival preservation
- Integration with Zenodo APIs or services

**No publication** — Layer-2 does not archive research materials.

## AI Execution or Automation

**PROHIBITED:**
- Execution of AI models or algorithms
- Deployment of AI agents or autonomous systems
- Running inference or prediction services
- Training or fine-tuning models
- Real-time AI processing or analysis
- Automated decision-making systems
- AI-powered content generation for scientific purposes

**No execution** — Layer-2 runs no AI systems.

**No authority** — Layer-2 cannot operate autonomous systems.

## Metrics, Benchmarks, Datasets

**PROHIBITED:**
- Publication of performance metrics
- Reporting of benchmark results
- Distribution of experimental datasets
- Presentation of comparative analyses
- Display of quantitative research outcomes
- Sharing of raw or processed data
- Publication of statistical results

**No results** — Layer-2 publishes no experimental data.

**No authority** — Layer-2 cannot validate or present research metrics.

## Analysis

**PROHIBITED:**
- Conducting any form of data analysis
- Performing statistical or computational analysis
- Executing analytical procedures or workflows
- Generating analytical insights or findings
- Interpreting analytical results
- Producing analytical reports or summaries

**Non-scientific** — Layer-2 performs no analysis.

**No authority** — Layer-2 has no analytical capabilities.

## Additional Prohibitions

**PROHIBITED:**
- Code execution or computational processing
- Data validation or verification
- Technical implementation or development
- Algorithm design or optimization
- System architecture for research purposes
- Infrastructure for scientific computing
- Integration with research tools or platforms
- Version control of research artifacts
- Release management for research outputs
- Distribution of scientific software

## Terminology Requirements

All documentation in this repository must consistently use:

- **Presentation-only**
- **Non-scientific**
- **No publication**
- **No citation**
- **No results**
- **No authority**

## Enforcement

These exclusions are **mandatory** and **non-negotiable**. Any content or activity that violates these prohibitions must be immediately removed or terminated.

Violations of these exclusions constitute a breach of Layer-2 boundaries and are strictly prohibited under TRIZEL governance.
